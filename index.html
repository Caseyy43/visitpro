Import Sales File

Drop your weekly FT Sales Report here
Supports .xlsb, .xlsx — filters Terr14 automatically
How importing works
1. Download your weekly FT Sales Report as received (the .xlsb file).
2. Click the upload area above and select the file.
3. VisitPro reads the Sales and Target Data sheet, filters to Terr14, matches your 44 stores, and updates the Sales tab.
4. Previous weeks are retained — you can switch between weeks using the dropdown on the Sales tab.

Note: Because .xlsb is a binary format that can't be read directly in the browser, you can also export the Sales and Target Data sheet to .xlsx or .csv and upload that instead.
